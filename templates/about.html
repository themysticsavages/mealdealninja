    • ABOUT US

    MEAL DEAL NINJA is about making nuritional, affordable recipes for those who struggle with FOOD INSECURITY. We focus on recipes that can range from vegan, dairy free, and gluten free. The range of recipes also provide the amount of calories the meal is. Our main motivation to create a program was due to the inaccessiable food options for those who can not afford to a sustainable food plan. Food insercurity is a serious health issue that is not commonly talked about in the media. We hope to raise awareness to this health issue and help people overcome their inaccessiability to healthy and nuritional food. Check out this link to find more about food insecurity.
